Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 26, 2025

This app helps with amateur radio “fox hunting” (ARDF) by letting you record and visualize radio bearings on a map.

What we collect

  • Location (device GPS): used to place bearings and show your position. Data stays on your device.
  • App data you create: bearings, colors, cached map tiles—stored locally on your device.
  • No accounts / no tracking: we don’t collect personal identifiers or track you across apps/sites.

Third-party services

Map layers may receive standard technical info (e.g., IP address) when tiles are requested:

  • Apple Maps (MapKit) — per Apple’s terms.
  • OpenStreetMap — © OpenStreetMap contributors (ODbL).
  • USGS Topographic maps — U.S. public domain.

Offline caching

If enabled, map tiles are saved on your device for offline use.

Your choices

You can deny Location permission (some features limited). You can delete bearings and clear cached tiles in the app.

Children’s privacy

General-audience app; we do not knowingly collect personal info from children.
